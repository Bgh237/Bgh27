Complete the Course

My main objective when it comes to JavaScript is to complete the course. We are currently about half way through the course and everything has gone well. We only have a few more subjects to cover and then it will be time for the final exam. Everything so far has been good, I have enjoyed learning JavaScript as it provides a bit more of a challenege than HTML and CSS. My aim is to do as well as I possibly can in the final exam and get the best grade I possibly can on the course.

I have noticed from my studies that I am nowhere near a full understanding of the language. I would like to be in a position at the end of my studies to full understand the code they are offering as I find it frustrating not understanding aspects of a subject I am learning. Whether we will cover these in the next months on the course I do not know but I would like to understand it anyway.
