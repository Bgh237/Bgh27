Job Hunt

This may be a little premature as I am still in my first semester studying at Haaga-Helia but at some point in the next few years I will need to start looking for a job within the ICT field. I have decided to create a table to show what jobs are currently available, what skills they are looking for and any other information that may be listed on the job advertisment. A lot of these jobs are going to be unsuitable for me due to my experience and skill levels but it is always good to know where you need to be in order to achieve your goals. These jobs have been taken from a number of different websites and have been advertised within the last few months so all skills are skills currently needed in the job market here in Helsinki.
